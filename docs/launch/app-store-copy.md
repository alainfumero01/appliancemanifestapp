## Description
LoadScan helps appliance resellers and liquidation operators turn sticker photos into organized load manifests in minutes.

Photograph an appliance sticker, extract the model number, and build a clean manifest with pricing, quantities, and export-ready line items. LoadScan is designed for internal operations teams that need a faster way to document loads before resale.

### Key Features
- Scan appliance stickers with your iPhone camera or photo library
- Extract and normalize appliance model numbers
- Look up cached product details and MSRP
- Build manifests with quantities, pricing, and condition notes
- Export manifests for internal review and sales workflows
- Share access across enterprise team plans
