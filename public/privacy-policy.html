Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 15, 2026

LoadScan is operated by Alain Fumero. This Privacy Policy explains what information LoadScan collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

  • Email address and account credentials used to access LoadScan.
  • Appliance sticker photos, manifest titles, load references, product details, quantities, and exports you create in the app.
  • Organization membership and subscription entitlement data needed to manage plan access.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your account and keep your data synced across sessions.
  • To extract model numbers, retrieve pricing information, and generate manifests.
  • To enforce free-tier limits, subscription entitlements, and enterprise seat access.
  • To provide support and respond to user requests.

Payments

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. LoadScan does not receive your full payment card information.

Sharing

LoadScan does not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with service providers needed to operate the app, such as backend hosting, storage, and AI processing services used for sticker/model recognition and pricing lookup.

Data Retention

Account and manifest data is retained while your account remains active and for a reasonable period afterward as needed for legitimate business, security, and support purposes.

Your Choices

You may request account-related support or data deletion by contacting the support email listed on the LoadScan support page.
